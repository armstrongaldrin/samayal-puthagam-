Delicious Breakfast Ideas

Soft and Fluffy Idly

Enjoy these soft and fluffy idly, a traditional South Indian breakfast made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar for a wholesome meal.

How to Make Idly:

  1. Prepare idly batter by soaking rice and lentils overnight, grinding them into a smooth paste, and fermenting the batter.
  2. Pour the batter into idly molds and steam for about 10-12 minutes until cooked through.
  3. Once cooked, remove the idly from the molds and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Crispy Dosa

Savor the crispiness of dosa, a popular South Indian breakfast dish made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Enjoy it with coconut chutney and spicy potato masala.

How to Make Dosa:

  1. Prepare dosa batter by soaking rice and lentils overnight, grinding them into a smooth paste, and fermenting the batter.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet or dosa tawa over medium heat and pour a ladleful of batter onto the center.
  3. Spread the batter in a circular motion to form a thin layer and drizzle oil around the edges.
  4. Cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy, then flip it over and cook the other side for a minute.
  5. Remove the dosa from the tawa and serve hot with coconut chutney and potato masala.

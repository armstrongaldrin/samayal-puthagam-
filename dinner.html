Delicious Indian Dinner Ideas

Butter Chicken

Indulge in this creamy and rich chicken curry made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a tomato-based sauce enriched with butter and cream. Serve with naan or rice for a comforting dinner.

How to Make Butter Chicken:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in yogurt, lemon juice, and spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree, spices like garam masala, coriander, cumin, and chili powder, and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until it's tender and cooked through.
  5. Finish with butter, cream, and fenugreek leaves for added flavor and richness.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Veg Biryani

Enjoy this aromatic and flavorful rice dish loaded with mixed vegetables and fragrant spices. Serve with raita or salad for a satisfying vegetarian dinner option.

How to Make Veg Biryani:

  1. Parboil basmati rice and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté whole spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves.
  3. Add sliced onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies, and sauté until onions are golden brown.
  4. Add mixed vegetables, spices like biryani masala, turmeric, and chili powder, and cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Layer cooked rice and vegetable mixture in a pot, and steam until flavors are infused.
  6. Garnish with fried onions, nuts, and raisins, and serve hot with raita or salad.

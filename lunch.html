Delicious Indian Lunch Ideas

Palak Paneer

Indulge in this creamy and flavorful North Indian dish made with spinach (palak) and paneer (Indian cottage cheese). Serve with hot roti or rice for a satisfying lunch.

How to Make Palak Paneer:

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for a few minutes, then drain and blend into a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies until golden brown.
  3. Add the spinach puree to the pan along with spices like turmeric, coriander, cumin, and garam masala.
  4. Cook until the mixture thickens, then add cubed paneer and simmer for a few minutes until heated through.
  5. Garnish with cream and serve hot with roti or rice.

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Enjoy this spicy and tangy chickpea curry made with a blend of aromatic spices. Serve with rice or bhatura for a classic North Indian lunch.

How to Make Chole:

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight, then cook them in a pressure cooker until tender.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes, spices like coriander, cumin, turmeric, and garam masala, and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add cooked chickpeas along with water and simmer until the gravy thickens.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or bhatura.
